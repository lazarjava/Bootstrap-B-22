Our Features

It all began in 1989, when the Magnum Classic was launched in Europe as the first handheld ice cream aimed exclusively at adults. Magnum's premium Belgian chocolatiers pioneered a unique chocolate coating with the signature 'crack' to accompany a deliciously smooth vanilla ice cream.
